Our
Story

A Legacy of Open Learning and Empowerment

The Open Learning and Distance Education Centre (OLDC/CDU) was founded with a vision to make quality education accessible to learners from all walks of life. Since its inception, OLDC/CDU has been committed to breaking barriers, fostering lifelong learning, and equipping students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in a dynamic world.

Our journey began in [Foundation Year], when the idea of flexible, inclusive, and technology-driven education was still emerging in India. Recognizing the potential of distance learning, OLDC/CDU established itself as a pioneer in providing opportunities for higher education outside the conventional classroom.

Empowering learners through flexible programs, modern technology, and a commitment to accessible, high-quality education.
