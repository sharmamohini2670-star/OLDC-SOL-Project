DIRECTOR

PROF. PAYAL MAGO

“

Our mission is to develop an inclusive and innovative learning environment that aligns with our motto, उत्तिष्ठत जाग्रत प्राप्य वरान्निबोधत —“Arise, awake, and achieve the goal.” To achieve this, we have launched various skill-based programs designed to cater to your interests and pursuits.  Whether you are a working professional seeking to upskill, a student looking to explore new subjects, or someone eager to continue learning for personal growth, we have something for everyone. With the integration of advanced technology, we deliver high-quality education and improved facilities provided to our students, enhancing their learning experiences.  

”

The strength of OLDC/CDU lies in its collective leadership — a team of educators, administrators, and visionaries who share a common goal: to make quality education accessible to all. Our leadership structure ensures transparency, academic excellence, and operational efficiency, fostering a culture of innovation and inclusion across all levels of the institution.

At OLDC/CDU, leadership is not just about administration — it’s about empowering every learner and educator to contribute to a shared mission.

Our
Governance
Philosophy

OLDC/CDU operates through a well-defined governance model that integrates academic and administrative leadership under one framework. We uphold the values of integrity, inclusiveness, and innovation, ensuring that governance remains participatory, forward-thinking, and learner-focused.
