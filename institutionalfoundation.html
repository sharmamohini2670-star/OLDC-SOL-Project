& Core Values

We exist to bridge the gap between access and opportunity, ensuring that learning remains inclusive, flexible, and future-focused for every individual.

At OLDC/CDU, we don't just teach for today — we prepare learners for a lifetime of discovery.

INTEGRITY

Upholding transparency and ethical standards in all academic and administrative processes.

INNOVATION

Encouraging creativity and forward thinking in teaching, research, and technology.

INCLUSIVITY

Ensuring that every learner feels seen, supported, and valued and transforms lives and communities.

Open learning for all

Our academic philosophy is guided by the principle of open learning for all. We believe that education should adapt to the learner — not the other way around.

Through a blend of digital platforms, self-paced modules, interactive sessions, and mentorship, OLDC/CDU fosters a learning ecosystem where students take ownership of their growth. This approach empowers learners to think critically, explore independently, and apply knowledge practically.

Our Vision

To be a prominent centre of innovation in open and distance education, providing equitable and transformative learning experiences that empower students globally to achieve academic and professional success.

Our Mission

01

Provide flexible and high-quality education to the students

02

Implementing best teaching and learning methods based on modern technology

03

Provide students with hands-on experience with our skill-based courses

Key Milestones

Built on the principle that education should transcend geography, age, and circumstance, the institution began with a small team of educators determined to bring flexible learning opportunities to underserved communities across India.

2020

OLDC/CDU is established, marking the beginning of India’s modern open learning initiative OLDC/CDU was established in 2000 with a vision to democratize higher education through open and distance learning.

We believe that every stage of life presents a chance to learn, adapt, and grow. Our programs, workshops, and research initiatives are designed to help learners stay relevant, resilient, and resourceful in a rapidly changing world.
